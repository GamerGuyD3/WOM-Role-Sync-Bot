How It Works

1. Set Group ID

Use /groupid to link your server to your WOM group.

2. Link Roles

Use /linkrole to map group ranks to Discord roles.

3. Link Users

Use /linkuser to connect members to their RSNs.

Why Use WOM Role Sync?

Automated & Effortless

Keep your Discord roles perfectly in sync with your Wise Old Man group roles without any manual effort. The bot checks for updates every hour, ensuring your server always reflects your clan's latest hierarchy.

Simple & Intuitive

A few simple slash commands are all it takes to get your server set up. The intuitive /help command will guide you through every step of the process, making configuration a breeze.
