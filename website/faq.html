Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about the WOM Role Sync bot.

The WOM Role Sync bot is a Discord bot that automatically synchronizes roles between a Wise Old Man (WOM) group and a Discord server. It periodically checks for updates in WOM group memberships and assigns or removes Discord roles based on a user's role within the WOM group.

1. Invite the bot to your server.
2. Set your Wise Old Man Group ID with /groupid [your_group_id].
3. Map WOM roles to Discord roles with /linkrole [wom_role] [@discord_role].
4. Link Discord users to their RuneScape names with /linkuser [@user] [runescape_name].
For a more detailed guide, please refer to the bot's /help menu.

The bot runs an automatic role synchronization task every hour on the hour for all configured servers.

There are a few things to check:
- Ensure the bot's role is placed higher than the roles it needs to assign in your Discord server's role hierarchy.
- Make sure the members are correctly linked with their RSNs using /linkuser.
- Verify that your WOM group page is up-to-date. You may need to have a clan member with the WOM plugin sync the group from in-game.
